Our Story

More Than a Cup of Coffee

Saloon 3 was born from a simple belief: that a cafe should feel like a third place, not home, not work, but somewhere in between. Since 2019, we have been that place for Casablanca.

We source our beans from Ethiopia, Colombia, and Brazil, roasted weekly by our partner roastery. Our kitchen bakes fresh every morning, no shortcuts, no compromises.
